About FindMyJob

FindMyJob is a web scraper that is used to find new published jobs on the Internet from sites FoundIT, Naukri, Internshala ans TimesJob to have a updated list at a glance which siginificantly reduces time and effort to search for jobs. It uses BeautifulSoup library to scrape through these websites which are then stores and saved on an excel sheet.
